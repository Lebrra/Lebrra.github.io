RoboCo
Filament Games

RoboCo is a sandbox robotics game currently available on early access on Steam. The purpose of RoboCo is to give students or people of all ages access to the world of robotics.

I have been working on upcoming features and quality of life updates on RoboCo since Sept 2023. I have also been working on performance improvements for our upcoming major release!

People's Pie
Filament Games + iCivics

People's Pie is my first professionally released title! This educational game teaches kids the basics of how US government funds are utilized.

This was my first lead engineer project at Filament Games working with the fantastic people at iCivics.

People's Pie was awarded a GEE! Award in 2023 under the category Formal Learning.

- Summer 2022

Mindful Market™
Filament Games + Penumbra, Inc

Mindful Market™ is an application of the REAL® System developed by Penumbra, Inc, which utilizes VR for various therapy solutions. Mindful Market is a project built in Unreal that helps patients build cognitive skills through several minigames.

I did several maintenance and quality of life updates and also added a new feature that allows the patient to stand while playing any activity.

- Spring 2022

Escape From Valhalla
Study Abroad Program

Escape from Valhalla was one of two projects created during a collaboration with UW-Whitewater and HAN University students. Unfortunately, we were unable to travel to the Netherlands, and this project was completed entirely remotely.

I was one of two programming leads for this project. Leading a team of five people from universities, my team and I developed the player mechanics and UI. I developed the menu systems and inventory, and created a custom input manager to support multiple controller types.
