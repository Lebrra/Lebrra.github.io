Brick Break

What is This?

Brick Break is a game I created as my final proejct for CS559 at UW Madison!

How to Play

  • Use A and D or Left and Right arrows to move the paddle around the track
  • You can also Left Click anywhere to move the paddle to the closest point clicked

  • Press Space or Right Click to launch the ball
  • The ball velocity is impacted by a combination of the speed of the paddle and where on the paddle it was hit

  • Press ESC anytime to pause your game and access some settings
  • The game should also pause when inactive

Enjoy!
